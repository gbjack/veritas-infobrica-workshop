With its exceptional scalability, elasticity, and lifecycle management, AWS has become the leading public cloud services provider.

But as organizations rush to move workloads to the cloud, IT managers are challenged to ensure the resiliency of their applications before, during, and after migration.

Register for the new “Rev Up Your Resiliency” Workshop
Join experts from Veritas and InfoFabrica in an illuminating half-day workshop that will cover

• Planning and implementing workload migration to the cloud, while achieving predictable application resiliency both in the cloud and on premises. This session will include demonstrations of both workload migration to AWS and managing application resiliency.
• Managing data protection across on-premises and AWS environments: How to develop a tiered strategy that provides seamless data protection across on-premises and cloud environments by leveraging a variety of data protection capabilities.
• Using managed services to protect data in the cloud: InfoFabrica (a Veritas and AWS partner) will present its approach to cloud backup and disaster recovery, including its commitment to SLAs that frees up enterprise IT teams to add greater value to their organizations.
Take the prudent path to cloud success.
